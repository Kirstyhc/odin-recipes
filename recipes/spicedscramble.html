Spiced Scrambled Eggs


These scrambled eggs are savoury and flavourful. They make an excellent weekend breakfast.
Serve with warm roti or chapatti and follow with some fresh mango!

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 a bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/2 a green chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Heat 1tbsp ghee or neutral oil in a large frying pan. Once hot, add the vegetables and stir. Cook, stirring occasionally, until softened and slightly browned.
  2. Whisk the eggs with the spices. Lower the heat in the pan and slowly add the whisked eggs.
  3. Cook slowly, stirring constantly, over a low heat until the eggs are cooked - about 3 minutes.
