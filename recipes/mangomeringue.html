Mango with Meringue


Juicy, ripe mango; crunchy, chewy meringues.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes - ideally Alphonse (also known as honey) mangoes.
  • 1 large meringue per person, ideally homemade.
  • 250ml double cream, whipped
  • (optional) chaat masala

Method

  1. Place one meringue on each plate.
  2. Add the double cream on the side.
  3. Optionally, sprinkle the mango with chaat masala.
  4. Peel and chop the mango. Add 1/2 cup chopped mango to each.
